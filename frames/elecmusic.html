Electronic Music

A New 2013-2014 Offering

Create original remixes, acoustic recordings, video-game scores, electronica, commercial film music, pop music, and more while learing Logic-Pro, Apple's flagship music production software. This course focuses on developing your own sounds and artistic identity using industry standard digital audio workstation software.

No musical experience is necessary, but basic instrument proficiency or a strong interest in music is needed. Students who have taken or are taking Sound System Design are strongly encouraged to enroll in Electronic Music.
