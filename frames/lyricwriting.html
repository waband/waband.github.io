Lyric Writing

This semester course is open to all students and focuses on the lyrical aspects of songwriting, and will seek to exercise creative writing practices in the context of composition. Examining lyric writing through song composition, structure, and form will provide a set of frameworks in which to develop creative writing abilities. This course will use aspects of music to help students better understand the rhythmic aspects of poetry, and will help students blend music and poetic concepts into a single expressive form.
