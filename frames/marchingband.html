Marching Band

Click here to sign up for the 2014-2015 marching band season. A recording of the show is available for marching band members here. Please contact Mr. Arsenault at garsenault@westfordk12.us for parts.

Westford Academy has one marching band that plays at football games. The marching band performs during halftime and plays songs from the stands during the game. The 2014-2015 halftime show is "Pirates of the Caribbean".

Marching band is subject to the school-wide activities fee. If you have not already paid this to another club, be sure to bring in a check at the beginning of the season.
