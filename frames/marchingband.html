Marching Band

Westford Academy has one marching band that plays at football games. The marching band performs during halftime and plays songs from the stands during the game. The 2013-2014 halftime show is "Elvis!". Their first performance of the year will be on Friday, September 6.

The marching band has purchased new uniforms this year. The coats for these uniforms have arrived, and other parts should be coming in the near future.
