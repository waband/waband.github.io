Marching Band

Click here to sign up for the 2015-2016 marching band season.

Westford Academy has one marching band that plays at football games. The marching band performs during halftime and plays songs from the stands during the game. The 2015-2016 halftime show is The Who's "Tommy".

Marching band is subject to the school-wide activities fee. If you have not already paid this to another club, be sure to bring in a check at the beginning of the season.
