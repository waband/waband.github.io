Video Submission Guidelines

All video submissions for playing assessments must be uploaded to Google Drive and shared with Mr. Arsenault. For each assignment, you are welcome to submit a cut-and-paste version of your video, but each assessment must be combined into a single video unless otherwise noted. This video does not need to be in an extremely high resolution; medium to large is fine. Free movie software should be more than capable of creating such a video. If you cannot do this at home, you may work on the project on one of the music department computers at school.

The video must be shared with either garsenault@westfordk12.us or westfordacademybands@gmail.com.

You must continue to host the files on your own Google Drive account for your entire career at WA. This is your four year portfolio; if you delete any files, you delete my copy as well. I do not have enough storage for 120 students, so this is an important responsibility. I encourage you to create a folder to store all of these files. The suggested name for this folder is NAME_Band_Portfolio.

You should name the recording and indicate what assignment you are recording (e.g. Arsenault-midterm-2013).
